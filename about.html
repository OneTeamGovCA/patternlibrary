About the OneTeamGov Canada Pattern Library

The idea in brief

The pattern library represents collective efforts by public servants across Canada to translate aspirations in the digital standards into practical, reusable policy tools to help teams succeed.

We are particularly interested in these types of tools to help accelerate the work of digital government teams:

  • Governance and accountability tools, for example role accountability matrices, digital governance charters and terms of reference, governance planning and reporting templates
  • Service and user-centred design tools, such as user research consent and compensation forms, service review checklists, service standards templates
  • Procurement and contracting tools, such as sample procurement and contracting clauses, vendor assessment checklists
  • Talent, culture and capability tools, for example job descriptions for common digital jobs, career pathways, learning and development, change management and digital adoption tools
  • Metrics, evaluation and continuous improvement tools, including dashboard templates, service review frameworks, impact assessment toolkits and user feedback templates

Criteria for inclusion in the library

We use the following filter to determine what gets included in the library:

  • Relevance - Does the pattern address a recurring policy problem faced by digital government teams in Canada? Would it solve a problem across multiple jurisdictions?
  • Application - Is the pattern currently being used by at least one order of government in Caanda? Are there lessons learned?
  • Ownership / Stewardship - Is the pattern being actively maintained by a team in a Canadian government (federal, provincial, territorial, regional or municipal?)
  • Licence - Is the pattern available for reuse using an open source licence?
  • metrics, service-standards, dashboards, monitoring frameworks
  • infrastructure or patterns (for e.g., API governance, cloud use, data-governance)
  • examples to help teams embed policy compliance (rather than just passive “must abide by” documents)

The OneTeamGov Canada Pattern Library is a community

The OneTeamGov Canada Pattern Library is a collaboration between the Governments of Canada, Alberta, British Columbia, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia and the Yukon Territory. Together, we represent a community of public servants committed to supporting government. Share your thoughts and suggestions with us by e-mail servicedigital-servicesnumerique@tbs-sct.gc.ca.
